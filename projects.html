Here's a collection of creative projects I've worked on, including game design, animation, and interactive media. Each project represents a unique challenge and a chance to express my ideas.

1st Place Newcomer Award

Garden Simulation Game

A cozy garden simulation game developed collaboratively with a team. I was responsible for all programming using a visual logic-based game maker. The game features a comprehensive plant growing system with weather and day/night cycles, a cooking system with 50 ingredients and 90 recipes, warehouse management, a gacha card system, maritime trading, mini-games, and more. Won 1st place in the platform's Newcomer Award.

Creative Project

Slam Poem Animation: "How I Struggle on Homework"

A creative multimedia project combining poetry, illustration, and voice acting. I wrote this slam poem for my Creative Writing class, where we were required to perform our poems in front of the class. As an introvert, I decided to create an animated video instead—letting it perform for me! I hand-drew all the animation frames and recorded my own voice-over. The video explores the universal student experience of struggling with homework through a blend of humor and relatability.

Interactive Presentation: Elements of a Good Design

A creative English assignment transformed into a fully interactive, game-like slideshow. Originally featured voice-overs, background music, and click sound effects— unfortunately, after graduating and losing access to my school account, the audio features are no longer available due to permission restrictions, but the interactive experience can still be viewed.
